Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common inquiries regarding our products, customization, and sourcing services.

Products & Customization
What are your primary product categories?
We specialize in three main pillars: Premium Home & Architectural Lighting (including modern fan lights, chandeliers, and table lamps), High-End Holiday Decorations (such as PE/PVC artificial Christmas trees, wreaths, and garlands), and Seasonal Production Raw Materials.
Do you support OEM/ODM and custom branding?
Yes, absolutely. As a dedicated B2B sourcing partner, we fully support custom specifications, material modifications, tailored packaging, and private labeling to align with your brand's unique market positioning.
What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?
To support emerging brands and market testing strategies, we offer flexible low-MOQ parameters for small-scale wholesale. Specific MOQs vary by product category and customization requirements. Please contact us with your details for a tailored quote.
Supply Chain & Logistics
How do you guarantee product quality?
Quality is our baseline. We operate strict pre-shipment inspection protocols. Our team conducts rigorous supplier evaluations, material testing, and on-site visual/functional quality control checks before any container leaves the factory clusters.
What logistics services do you provide?
We handle seamless end-to-end global supply chain integration. This includes factory-direct price negotiation, continuous production monitoring, complex customs clearance documentation, and reliable global freight forwarding/shipping coordination.
